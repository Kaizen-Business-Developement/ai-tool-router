Which AI should

No single AI does everything. The operators who get the most out of AI use each tool for what it's best at, and they let Profit Mind AI make the business decisions the others can't. Here's the map.

The principle

One rule keeps it clean.

If it's about you, not the business, it goes to Perplexity.

Your business AIs (Profit Mind AI, Claude, ChatGPT) only ever see business context. Everything personal, and any throwaway fact-check, stays in Perplexity, out of their memory. That's the whole reason for the split.

In practice

The loops you actually run.

Real work bounces between tools. These are the common round-trips.

Profit Mind AI→ Perplexity→ Claude Cowork

Strategise → verify → build. A plan makes a claim; check the number or market fact before you commit, then build it out.

ChatGPT Work→ Claude Cowork

Research → build. Set ChatGPT Work running, then bring the findings into Cowork to actually make something with them.

Claude Cowork→ Profit Mind AI→ Claude Cowork

Build → rethink → build. Mid task you hit a strategic fork, so you step out to Profit Mind AI for the call, then back to Cowork to carry on.

The stack at a glance

What each tool is for.

Perplexity

Personal

Random questions, health, shopping, travel, curiosity, plus quick sourced fact-checks on either side.

Best for: fast, cited answers

Profit Mind AI

Think

Business strategy, planning, and judgment calls. 18 specialist brains that reason commercially, unlike the general models.

Best for: business thinking

Claude Cowork

Do · daily driver

Your daily driver for getting work done. Cowork works your files, folders and apps and hands back finished docs, sheets and decks. No setup and nothing technical.

Best for: getting work done

ChatGPT Work

Research · agent

ChatGPT Work is ChatGPT's agent mode. It connects to your apps and runs long, multi step jobs in the background, then delivers finished research, sheets or slides. Not casual chat.

Best for: long jobs you leave running

Building actual software is a different job: that is Claude Code and ChatGPT Codex. This guide sticks to Cowork and Work, the tools most people need.
